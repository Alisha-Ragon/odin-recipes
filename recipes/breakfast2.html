Rice and Sausage

Ingredients

  • 4c flour
  • 4 Tbsp baking powder
  • 4c warm milk
  • 3/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 4 egg whites

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together leaving a well / hole in the center.
  2. Add melted butter, warm milk and egg yolks to the well and stir to combine.
  3. Once wet ingredients are mixed, incorporate into the dry ingredients.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk egg whites to achieve soft peaks
  5. Add the beaten egg whites to the batter and gently fold to combine.
  6. Heat a skillet over low heat. Cook 1.5 cups batter at a time covered, roughly 20 minutes each, until brown on the bottom
  7. Top with syrup of your choice or other garnish if desired. Enjoy!
