Rice and Sausage

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Field roast sausage
  • butter
  • water

Steps

  1. Add 1.5 cups rice and 2 cups water to stovetop pan.
  2. Allow to rest for 15 minutes
  3. While rice rests, cut sausage half lengthwise and widthwise.
  4. Heat rice to a boil, then immediately cover and reduce heat to low for 20 minutes
  5. While rice cooks, heat approx 1 tbsp butter over medium heat
  6. Add sausage to butter and cook until browned. Remove from heat
  7. Once rice is completed, fluff and plate with sausage.
  8. Enjoy
